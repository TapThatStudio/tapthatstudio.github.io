Before you book

Easy to plan. Easy to show up.

Where do we meet?

Most EDventure sessions meet at Castle Creek Boat Ramp on Lake Pleasant. Captain Ed will confirm the exact meet-up details after booking.

What should we bring?

Swimwear, towels, sunscreen lotion, snacks, drinks, and whatever keeps your crew happy.

Is this beginner friendly?

Yes. Captain Ed works with first-timers, families, and experienced riders.

What about weather?

Lake conditions matter. If weather becomes an issue, Captain Ed will help coordinate next steps.

Three tiny rules

Keep the boat pretty. Keep the day smooth.

01

Kick off the kicks

No shoes on the boat. Barefoot lake luxury.

02

Sunscreen smart

Lotion is best. Spray only on the swim platform unless you enjoy slippery chaos.

03

No cheesy dust

Cheetos and white vinyl are not emotionally mature enough to coexist.

Life vests are provided and required when appropriate. BYOB is welcome for guests 21+, and Captain Ed keeps the day safe, smooth, and captain-led.
